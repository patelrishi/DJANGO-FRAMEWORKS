About Us

RayScan is young Startup with passion to develop approachable solution to global health issue.
With our innovative technology, we help radiologists to have diagnosis results hands-on in a fraction of time. The objective of our product is not limited to help the radiologists but it also serves as an effective solution in the pandemic these days. By using Artificial Intelligence, our application can detect the symptoms of lungs-related diseases just by uploading X-Ray and CT scan images.

Services

Hands-on report

Our product uses Artificial Intelligence and the latest technology to provide patients with diagnosis reports in a fraction of seconds.

Cloud-based storage

We also provide cloud storage to our users where they can store all their previous reports and refer to them in the future anytime and anywhere they want.

Results in a fraction of seconds

Our app is embedded with modern-day technology to provide its users with accurate results in a fraction of seconds.

Cost-effective

RayScan is a very economical product and the fees charged is very customer-friendly.

Detailed Screening

Our screening technology has the ability to detect more than 12 lungs-diseases like Covid-19, pneumonia etc.

Easy to Analyse

Our system not just provides the diagnosis reports but also detect and mark the infected areas within the reports for easy understanding of users.
